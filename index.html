Yuancheng Zhu （朱远骋）

I was a Shepp Research Fellow and Lecturer in the Statistics Department, Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania from 2016 to 2018.

I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. John Lafferty and received my Ph.D. degree in Statistics at the University of Chicago.

Before coming to Chicago, I received my BS in mathematics at Fudan University.

I am broadly interested in the intersection of statistics, machine learning, and optimization.
